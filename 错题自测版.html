一、括号内词的「词性 / 词形」判断错误 9 题

篇错题所在句子正确答案考点
③…these unassuming walls have a ___(charm) story spanning thousands of years.charming修饰名词 story → 形容词 charming
③…these structures stand as a true ___(represent) of the resilience and development of Chinese history.representationa + true 后需名词;represent→representation
④Goodall's lifelong work demonstrates that ___(meaning) environmental protection requires genuine commitment…meaningful修饰名词 protection → 形容词
⑦…some of which reflect the ___(struggle) and growth of ordinary people.strugglesstruggle 本身即名词(勿生造词),与 growth 并列
⑦Labubu's appeal spans Asia, with long lines at releases and extensive ___(cover) in major media like BBC.coverageextensive 后需名词;cover→coverage(报道)(coverage 属超纲词,了解即可)
⑩…vultures play a critical role in maintaining environmental hygiene and biodiversity ___(stable)…stabilitymaintain 的宾语需名词;stable→stability
⑫…a sense of connection between people and things, and even to the far ___(expand) natural world.more expansive修饰 natural world → 形容词;far more + 比较级
⑫…to better reflect the ___(unit) of people and things, and people and nature.unitythe 后需名词;辨析 unit(单位)≠ unity(统一)
②China proposed ___(priority) for future SCO agricultural cooperation…prioritiespriority 为可数名词,泛指多项 → 复数(且 to 多余)
